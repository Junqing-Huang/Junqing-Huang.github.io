Junqing Huang

Department of Mathematics, Ghent University

Building S8, Krijgslaan 281, B 9000 Ghent, Belgium

I am a Ph.D. Student at Ghent Analysis & PDE Center, Ghent University, Belgium, supervised by Prof.Michael Ruzhansky and Prof. Haihui Wang (Beihang University, BUAA). I received my Master’s degree in Applied Mathematics, from Beihang University, in 2015.

Before joining into Ghent Analysis & PDE Center, I also had 4 years of valuable engineering experience, working as a research assistant at the School of Mathematics, Beihang University from March 2018 to March 2019, as a Research Engineer, at the Media Lab, Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. from August 2015 to March 2018, and as a Research Intern, at the Intel China Research Center Ltd. from November 2014 to July 2015, respectively.

My research interests are mainly rooted in the inter-discipline of applied mathematics and artificial intelligence, with special focuses on signal/image processing, computer vision and graphics, and deep learning, as well as the connections to mathematical backgrounds such as PDE, variational analysis, wavelet and sparse theory, optimization theory and algorithm, optimal transport theory, and so on.

Feel free to contact me via junqing.huang@ugent.be or buaahjq@163.com, if you are interested in any collaborations or have any suggestions.
